About Lead Latch

Lead Latch is a project I am building to help local service businesses keep track of their leads, quotes, follow-ups, and completed jobs. I like this idea because many small business owners lose money when they forget to reply to customers or follow up after giving a quote. A fun fact about this project is that it works like a simple CRM, but it is designed to be easier and less overwhelming for local business owners.

Project Features

The main purpose of Lead Latch is to help business owners stay organized. The project focuses on lead tracking, quote follow-ups, job status updates, and review requests after a job is finished. These features are useful because they help a business respond faster and avoid losing potential customers.

Lead tracking project for local service businesses

Lead Latch helps small businesses keep every lead in sight.

Lead Latch is a simple CRM project I am building to help local service businesses track customer leads, quotes, follow-ups, completed jobs, and review requests without making the process feel overwhelming.

See the features View brand style

Overview

A focused CRM idea with less clutter

Many small business owners lose money when they forget to reply to a customer or fail to follow up after giving a quote. Lead Latch is designed around that problem. Instead of trying to be a large business platform, the goal is to give local service owners a clear place to manage the customer work that matters most.

Core purpose

Built around the daily workflow of local service businesses

01

Capture new leads

Keep customer requests organized so business owners can respond before the opportunity goes cold.

02

Track quotes and follow-ups

Make it easier to see which customers still need a reply, estimate, or follow-up message.

03

Close jobs and request reviews

Use completed job information to help the business stay organized and ask customers for reviews at the right time.

Visual identity

Clean, modern, and practical

The Lead Latch design uses a mint background, dark navy text, and teal accents to create a clean and professional style. Rounded cards, pill-shaped buttons, and consistent spacing help the site feel organized and easy to follow.

✓

Consistent colors

Teal, mint, navy, and white are repeated across the pages to make the brand feel connected.

Aa

Readable typography

Strong headings and simple body text make the content clear for small business owners.

↗

Unified layout

Cards, buttons, icons, and spacing use the same style across the full site.

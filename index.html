Lead Latch helps small businesses keep every lead in sight.

Lead Latch is a simple CRM project I am building to help local service businesses track customer leads, quotes, follow-ups, completed jobs, and review requests without making the process feel overwhelming.

See the features Open current build

Overview

A focused CRM idea with less clutter

Many small business owners lose money when they forget to reply to a customer or fail to follow up after giving a quote. Lead Latch is designed around that problem. Instead of trying to be a large business platform, the goal is to give local service owners a clear place to manage the customer work that matters most.

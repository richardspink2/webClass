Japan-Inspired Design

Color and structure were adapted for a Japanese audience.

Blue supports trust, red adds energy, white keeps the page clean, and gold acts as a success accent.

Color Palette

Four colors were chosen for their cultural meaning.

Red

Represents energy and celebration.

White

Supports clarity and cleanliness.

Blue

Communicates trust and reliability.

Gold

Adds a success and quality accent.

Localization Choices

The redesign changed more than color.

  • Language: This class version keeps the writing in English for readability.
  • Information density: Company details, business hours, and reply expectations appear earlier.
  • Typography: The page uses Noto Sans JP and M PLUS 1p for a cleaner Japan-inspired look.
  • Navigation: Contact access stays visible throughout the site.

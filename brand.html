Brand Style

Clean, calm, and practical for small business users.

The brand style uses a simple palette, readable typography, and matching imagery so the pages feel connected and easy to use.

Color Palette

Four colors set the mood of the site.

Deep navy

Used for headings and strong contrast.

Teal

Used for buttons, links, and section labels.

Mint

Used in the background for a calm, clean feel.

Clay

Used as a soft accent so the design does not feel flat.

Style Notes

Short post summary

Typography

Sora is used for headings and Manrope is used for body text for a clear hierarchy.

Imagery

All images show work, teamwork, or organization so they match the purpose of the site.

Consistency

The same spacing, cards, and buttons are repeated across all three pages.

Lead Latch uses navy, teal, mint, and clay to feel trustworthy, calm, and practical for local service businesses. Sora headings and Manrope body text keep the pages readable, and the images all share the same clean business style. These choices fit the audience because the site is meant to help busy small business owners stay organized without feeling overwhelmed.

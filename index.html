Simple CRM project

Lead Latch keeps local service leads organized.

This project is built for small service businesses that need one clean place to track leads, quotes, follow-ups, jobs, and review requests.

View Features View Brand Style

Project Goal

A focused CRM idea without extra clutter.

Lead Latch is meant to solve a simple problem: small business owners lose work when they forget to reply, follow up, or send a review request. The design stays simple so the site feels calm and easy to scan.

Features

Built around the daily workflow of a service business.

The feature list is small on purpose. It focuses on the work a local service business does every day instead of trying to be a large corporate platform.

Core Tools

The main tasks stay visible and easy to manage.

LD

Capture leads

Store new customer requests in one place before they get lost.

QT

Track quotes

See who has received an estimate and who still needs a reply.

RV

Request reviews

Follow up after the job is done and ask for customer feedback.

Workflow

The process follows a simple four-step path.

  • 1. Capture the lead and save the customer details.
  • 2. Send the quote and mark who needs a follow-up.
  • 3. Move the customer into the job stage when they accept.
  • 4. Finish the job and request a review at the right time.

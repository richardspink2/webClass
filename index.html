Local service business CRM concept

Keep service leads from slipping through the cracks.

Lead Latch is a project I am building to help local service businesses organize leads, quotes, follow-ups, completed jobs, and review requests in one simple place.

About the project

Built for small businesses that depend on follow-ups

Many small business owners lose money when they forget to reply to a customer or fail to follow up after sending a quote. Lead Latch is meant to work like a simple CRM, but with less clutter and a clearer focus on local service work.

Project features

Core tools for lead tracking

Lead Capture

Store new customer requests so business owners can respond before the opportunity goes cold.

Quote Follow-Ups

Track quote value and follow-up status so potential jobs are easier to manage.

Completed Jobs

Keep a record of finished work and use that information to request reviews from customers.
